Fabulist

Fabulist is a collaborative story telling game in the style of Exquisite Corpse, the parlor game Consequences and Mad Libs. Any number of people can play. Fabulist is a game where participants take turns writing paragraphs of a story. If a player wants, they can draw an illustration in their turn instead of writing a paragraph. After each player has gone three times, Fabulist stitches the parts together into one story. Hilarity ensues.
